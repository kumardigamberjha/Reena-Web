Tinting

By shaping and tinting your eyelashes and brows to suit the contours of your face and colour of your hair you can bring out the beauty in your eyes and transform your look.

Lash Tint

Your eyelashes are tinted with a healthy, glossy and natural finish, replacing the need to use mascara, this will help them to stand out and frame your eyes.

Brow Tint

Eyebrows come in many shapes and forms and a good eyebrow shape can frame the face and give an instant lifted appearance. A tint can work wonders as our natural eyebrow colour can fade with age. A tint even just a shade or two deeper or darker can instantly take years off us. Of course for those of us who want the darkest of dark defined eyebrows we can do that too.

Eyelash Perming

Eyelash perm will allow your straight lashes to become curved eyelashes, which look great and your eyes will appear bigger.

Eyelash Extensions

Lash extensions - that look very natural are joined to your own lash. You can keep the long lash illusion going for ages. Lasts up to 6 weeks.
